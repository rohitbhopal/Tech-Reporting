Three User Roles

Designed with role-based access to meet the needs of your entire field service team.

Technician

Field workers performing jobs

  • View assigned jobs and clock in/out
  • Submit reports with photos and signatures
  • Track work hours with timesheets
  • Receive real-time notifications
  • Access job history and documentation

Lead

Team managers overseeing work

  • Assign jobs to technicians
  • Monitor team performance and job status
  • View and export all reports
  • Manage schedules and priorities
  • Generate performance analytics

Admin

System administrators with full access

  • Manage users and permissions
  • Configure system settings
  • Access all data and analytics
  • Customize forms and workflows
  • Integrate with other business systems

App Details

Everything you need to manage field jobs efficiently.

Location Tracking

Capture location data when submitting reports and view job locations on interactive maps.

Photo Uploads

Capture before and after photos for job documentation and quality assurance.

Time Tracking

Clock in and out of jobs with automatic timesheet generation and reporting.

Export Reports

Generate PDF reports with photos, signatures, and job details for clients and records.

Offline Mode

Continue working without internet connection with automatic data synchronization when back online.

Digital Signatures

Capture customer signatures on mobile devices for service verification and approval.

Role-Based Dashboards

Custom dashboards for each role with relevant information and quick actions.

  • • Lead: Team overview and job assignment
  • • Admin: User management and system overview

Job Details Screen

Report Submission

Intuitive form for submitting job reports with before/after photos, notes, and signature capture.

Users

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about the Field Job Management app.

What is the Manus AI App Builder?

Manus is a no-code platform that allows you to build custom mobile and web applications without writing any code. It uses AI to help you design and implement complex business applications quickly and efficiently.

Do I need programming skills to use this app?

No programming skills are required. The Manus-ready JSON file contains all the necessary configuration, and the Manus platform provides a visual interface for any customizations you might want to make.

Can I customize the app to match my business needs?

Yes, the app is fully customizable. You can modify field names, add or remove features, change the branding, and adjust workflows to match your specific business processes using the Manus platform.

Does the app work offline?

Yes, the app includes offline functionality. Technicians can continue working without an internet connection, and all data will automatically synchronize when they're back online.

How secure is the data in the app?

The app uses industry-standard security practices including encrypted data storage, secure authentication, and role-based access controls to ensure your data remains protected.

Can I integrate this app with other business systems?

Yes, the Manus platform supports integration with various business systems through its API capabilities. You can connect to CRM systems, accounting software, and other tools to create a seamless workflow.
